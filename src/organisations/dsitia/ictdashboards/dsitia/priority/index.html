Priority

Our ICT-enabled initiatives are prioritised as:

  • critical—mandatory initiatives essential to the delivery of Queensland Government objectives, major legislative or ministerial requirements, or major policy initiatives with very high risk, complexity, cost and benefits
  • high—important initiatives but not essential to major legislative or policy initiatives with high risk, complexity, cost and benefits
  • medium—initiatives with relatively high attractiveness and achievability scores but that do not fall within the definition of critical or high initiatives
  • low—initiatives which improve business efficiency and management effectiveness with low cost, risk and benefits.
