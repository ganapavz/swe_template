Welcome to the Queensland Digital Economy Strategy - online engagement space.

This community engagement portal aims to provide an interactive space for interested Queenslanders to find out more, converse and contribute to the draft Digital Economy Strategy. You can help shape the strategy by accessing information and giving your feedback and advice online through surveys, quick polls, stories, discussion forums and submissions. 

The Queensland Government is acutely aware of the rapid changes and opportunities that are occurring in the Digital Economy.  Our vision is that Queensland will be Australia’s most digitally interactive state and recognised globally as a digital innovation hub. 

The Strategy is being developed to ensure that our state, including government and non-government organisations and all Queenslanders, is best positioned to enjoy the economic and social benefits of the digital economy.

Your advice and feedback will help inform and shape the Queensland Digital Economy.
