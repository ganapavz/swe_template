Enter the 2012 Queensland Premier’s Literary Awards

To enter:

  • Provide details of the entry through the form below.
  • Print a copy of the entry form — don’t forget it needs to be signed by the author
  • Post copies of the work and the entry form to:

    Literary Awards Coordinator
    Events Coordination
    Department of the Premier and Cabinet
    PO Box 15185
    City East QLD 4002

  • Or deliver to:

    Literary Awards Coordinator
    Events Coordination
    Department of the Premier and Cabinet
    Level 2, Executive Building
    100 George Street
    Brisbane

Please note:

  • Make sure all details are correct (including spelling, capitalisation and full titles of works and authors).
  • The entry must comply with the guidelines for the 2012 Queensland Premier’s Literary Awards.
  • You must include this official entry form. Without it, entries are not eligible.
  • You must use a separate entry form for each entry.
  • Copies of your work will not be returned.
  • Entries must be received by 5pm 11 May 2012.

  • Privacy information

    The Department of the Premier and Cabinet (the department) is collecting your personal information in accordance with the Information Privacy Act 2009 (Qld), in order to coordinate/ administer awards, events and initiatives for the department. The information will be stored securely and accessed only by the department’s authorised employees. Some of this information will be given to the 2012 Queensland Premier’s Literary Awards judging panel for the purpose of judging the awards, and shortlisted entrants’ details will be provided to Brisbane Writer’s Festival and University of Queensland Press for the purposes of publication, publicity and/or mentoring. Your personal information will not be disclosed to any other third party without your consent, unless authorised or required by law. If at any time you would like your personal information removed from our database, please send your request to literaryawards@premiers.qld.gov.au or to fax number (07) 3224 4065.
