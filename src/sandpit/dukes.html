Children aged under 11 years and 6 months old are too young to to join the Duke of Edinburgh Award. Once a child is over 11 years and 6 months old, but under 13 years and 9 months old, they can join the Queensland Bridge Award.

Children 11 years and 6 months to 13 years and 9 months are too young to join the Duke of Edinburgh Award, but can enroll in the Queensland Bridge Award.

People over the age of 25 are not elibible to join the Duke of Edinburgh Award
