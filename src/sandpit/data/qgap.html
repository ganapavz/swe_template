Government service office locations

Access a broad range of government services at our offices around the state.

In rural and smaller regional areas, visit a Queensland Government Agent Program (QGAP) office.

In major regional centres and the Brisbane CBD, visit a Queensland Government Service Counter.

These offices provide information, help and advice on all State Government services. Some offices also provide Commonwealth Government and Suncorp/NRMA insurance services.

Call your local office to find out if they offer the service you need.
