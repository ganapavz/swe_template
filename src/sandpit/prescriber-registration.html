Prescriber registration form

CAEATI welcomes your professionalism as you join our Registered CAEATI Prescriber (RCP) network. CAEATI aims to offer individualised support for people with a disability who want, or need assistance to fund equipment to enable access to their communities of choice.

Completing this Prescriber Registration Form By completing the following details you enable CAEATI to:

  • Develop a register of the prescriber workforce for dissemination of CAEATI information.
  • Develop a list of RCP’s to be available for referral.
  • Allow payments for service to occur (if applicable).
