Queensland Fire and Rescue Service - Assessment Booking

Fitness and Physical Abilities Assessments

The role of firefighter demands high levels of physical and cognitive ability, this is reflected in the assessments for the Queensland Fire and Rescue Service (QFRS) Basegrade Firefighter recruitment process.

Please ensure you have prepared well for all assessments to ensure you give yourself the best opportunity to be successful.

  • You must be an active applicant to book in for these assessments.
  • The cost for the assessments are:
    • Fitness Assessment - $20 (inc GST).
    • Physical Abilities Assessment - $50 (inc GST).
    • Total cost if booking for both - $70 (inc GST).
  • Your booking will be taken and you will receive a receipt of payment.
  • You will receive confirmation of your booking date/s and further information regarding the assessments directly from QFRS Recruitment.

It takes about 5 minutes to complete this form.
