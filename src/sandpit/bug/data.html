Premier’s Awards for Open Data

The Premier’s Awards for Open Data are now closed.

$5,000 in prize money will be awarded in each of the following categories to the winners that demonstrate the most innovative use of and real outcomes from public data:

  • Public transport—use that delivers real improvements to customer experience
  • Improved service delivery—use that delivers improved social services to Queenslanders
  • Growing and developing Queensland’s future—use that delivers real economic benefits to Queenslanders
  • Open—wildcard category.
