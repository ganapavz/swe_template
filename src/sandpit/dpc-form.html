2012 Queensland Premier’s Literary Awards Official entry form

The official entry form should be completed in accordance with the conditions of entry outlined in the award guidelines for the 2012 Queensland Premier's Literary Awards. Failure to comply with the award guidelines will render a nomination ineligible.

The official entry form and copies of entries should be sent to the following address, and must be received by 5pm 11 May 2012.

All works entered must be accompanied by a completed official entry form. Books or manuscripts or other entries in accordance with the award guidelines that are not accompanied by a completed official entry form will not be eligible.

A separate official entry form must be completed for each entry.

Copies of submitted works and manuscripts received will not be returned to the relevant authors or publishers. No correspondence will be entered into.
