Welcome to the Queensland Registry of Births, Deaths and Marriages historical index searching facility.

Click on:

  • "Search" - Provides the data fields to commence searching.
  • "Download Purchased Images" - Allows you to download image products from previous purchases.
  • "Search Tips" - Tips for searching the historical indexes.
  • "Help" - Detailed information about how to use BDM’s online ordering service.
  • "Contact" - Contact information for BDM.

Only life event records that are outside of Queensland BDM’s Access Policy can be searched. Access policy periods are:

  • Births occurring more than 100 years ago.
  • Deaths occurring more than 30 years ago, and
  • Marriages occurring more than 75 years ago.

No search results will be displayed if dates within the above period are entered

Life event records currently available to search:

  • Births up to and including 1914
  • Deaths up to and including 1964
  • Marriages up to and including 1937
