QPS ICMC Client Satisfaction Survey (Preview)

Welcome to the Queensland Police Service Intelligence, Counter-Terrorism
and Major Events Command Client Satisfaction Survey

What is the purpose of this survey?
The purpose of this survey is to collect information regarding the services provided by the Intelligence, Counter-Terrorism and Major Events Command (ICMC), including State Intelligence, Security and Counter-Terrorism Group and the Covert and Specialist Operations Group. The information you provide will be used, where appropriate, to improve the services provided by ICMC. It is intended this survey will be conducted bi-annually.

Am I required to identify myself?
The information you provide will be collated and disseminated in a way that results reported will not be able to be linked back to any individual. Your responses will be treated anonymously unless you indicate otherwise at the end of the survey.

Where can I obtain further information about this evaluation?
Should you require any additional information before participating in this survey, or you believe there is someone in your command/region/organisation who is better suited to complete the survey, please contact the ICMC Research Officer on telephone (07) 3364 4561 or email ICMCexternalaccount@police.qld.gov.au.

Do I have to participate?
Participation is encouraged, however is entirely voluntary.

Who will be invited to participate in this survey?
This survey will be distributed to persons within the Queensland Police Service and in external agencies who would be expected to have dealings with ICMC.

How long will the survey take to complete?
The survey should only take about 10 minutes of your time to complete.
