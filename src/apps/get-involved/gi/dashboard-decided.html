Child care laws review

From 1 January 2012, Queensland day care, family day care, outside school hours care and kindergarten services will be regulated under new national laws.

About 10,000 people had their say on child care in Queensland. We have decided to introduce the legislation, with some amendments based on community concerns. Read about the changes to the Child Care Act .
