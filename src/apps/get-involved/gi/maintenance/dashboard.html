Child care laws review

Do you have children in day care? How will these changes affect you?

Background

From 1 January 2012, Queensland day care, family day care, outside school hours care and kindergarten services will be regulated under new national laws.

Regulation of child care services is currently controlled under the Queensland Child Care Act 2002. These services include limited hours care services, occasional care services and certain licensed services receiving Commonwealth budget based funding.
