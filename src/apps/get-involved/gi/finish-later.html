About your privacy

The Department of Education is collecting the information contained in this response form to assist with the development of the Child Care Act 2002. Your personal information collected in this response form will be maintained in accordance with the Information Privacy Act 2009 and the Public Records Act 2002. Your submission may be provided to other agencies for comment during the finalisation of the Child Care Act 2002. Your personal information will not be provided to any other party without your consent or as authorised by law. If you have any inquiries about privacy, please contact the Privacy Contact Officer on +61 7 3225 8463. Your personal information will not be published on this site, even if you decide to publish your responses.
