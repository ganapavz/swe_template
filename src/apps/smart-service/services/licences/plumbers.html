Plumbers and drainers licence renewal

This form is for licensed plumbers and drainers to renew their licence in accordance with the Plumbing and Drainage Act 2002.

  • Enter your registration number and surname.
  • Choose a renewal term of one to five years.
  • We accept credit card and BPAY.
  • Questions marked with * must be completed.
  1. Pay the renewal fee
    3. Renewal term * $56 per year for up to five years (fees are effective from 1 July 2010)
    4. Processing fee All licence renewals attract a licence application processing fee $51.00
  2. Provide supporting documentation

    You must either provide your current plumbers and drainers licence, or download and complete Form 27 Proof of identity and document declaration form and attach two (2) passport sized photographs. Please print your name on the back of each photo.
