Designing URLs

Agencies must design URLs to last.

Benefits of conformance

A consistent, quality experience for users of the site. Increased site credibility and levels of user trust. Well designed URLs are more marketable and memorable.

Risks of non-conformance

Users encounter site errors with increased frequency. Possible increase in offline channel requests for inexplicably removed information. Reduced site credibility and levels of user trust.
