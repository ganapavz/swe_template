ICT dashboards

Welcome to the Queensland Government ICT dashboards.

These dashboards provide views for each Queensland Government agency’s information and communications technology (ICT) initiatives.

As part of our commitment to open and transparent government, we are publishing the original datasets supporting the ICT dashboards at the Queensland Government data website.

Publication of all agencies' dashboards was completed by the end of November 2013 to meet the commitment of the Queensland Government.

Each agency is responsible for refreshing their dashboard data in line with their reporting cycle and governance body approvals. The data contained on agency dashboards and the supporting datasets is owned by the agency.
