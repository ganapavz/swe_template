Seniors Week events

  1. "An Afternoon at Brookland"

    27 August 2011 1–3pm

    The concert is the culmination of a week of 'living, breathing history' activities held across Centacare - Amelia House Services. Hosted by Bruce Paige who will interview 'Joe Dacey', an original WWI 'dambuster', the afternoon musically celebrates seniors with three choirs, a solo performance and filmed interviews.

    Robertson, - (07) 3324 0888 - 27/08/2011

  2. Seniors Information Stalls

    22 August 2011 10am–3pm

    Stalls providing information on services available to Seniors in this region.

    Wynnum, - (07) 3348 6067 - 22/08/2011

  3. Seniors Week Celebration Worship Service

    20 August 2011 9.30am–11.15am

    A special worship service to honour seniors. Featuring much loved hymns.

    Atherton, - (07) 4091 5396 - 28/08/2011

  4. Seniors Club special Movie Preview screening of THE HELP with free Morning Tea served exclusively to members of the Event Cinemas and Birch Carroll & Coyle Cinemas Seniors Club

    24 August 2011 10am

    Enjoy a FREE Seniors Club Morning Tea on 24 August at 10am followed by a 10.30am advanced movie screening of THE HELP for just $8! Deeply moving, filled with poignancy, humour and hope, THE HELP (M) is a timeless and universal story about the ability to create change. Become a Seniors Club member to attend. Membership is free! Visit www.seniorsclubonline.com.au

    All over Qld, - (07) 3906 4600 - 24/08/2011

  5. Oak Tree Retirement Village Warwick - Italian Luncheon with Music and Bands

    29 August 2011 11.30am–2pm

    Celebrate Seniors Week with us as we showcase our Warwick retirement village and the newly completed community facilities (the largest of it's kind in the local area). Join our residents and enjoy a free afternoon including an Italian Feast by Brisbane's newest Italian experience - Fratelli, with live music, bands and singing.

    Warwick, - 1300367155 - 29/08/2011

  6. Launch of the Stanthorpe Community Garden

    28 August 2011 10am–4pm

    Launch of the Stanthorpe Community Garden with a range of workshops/activities: 10am-11am - Learn how to build a veggie garden from scratch; 11am-1pm - Sausage sizzle lunch ($2); 1pm-2pm - Learn about the wicker bed and no dig garden method; 2pm-4pm - Learn about compost making. All welcome.

    Stanthorpe Qld, - (07) 4681 3777 - 28/08/2011

  7. Computing made easier!

    20–28 August 2011

    Computing made easier! Come and try workshops on email, web editing, photo shop, podcasting, and computer basics. Workshops will be held on 20, 21, 22, 24, 27 & 28 August. Workshop times will be confirmed on booking. Bookings preferred.

    Stanthorpe Qld, - (07) 4681 4384 - 20/08/2011

    Stanthorpe Qld, - (07) 4681 2141 - 26/08/2011

  10. Heart Foundation Heartmoves Class

    26 August 2011 12–1pm

    Classes are FREE to new participants in Seniors Week! Heart Foundation Heartmoves is a gentle physical activity program suitable for anyone who hasn't done any exercise for a while, and is safe for people with health conditions like heart disease, diabetes and bone or joint problems.

    Stanthorpe Qld, - (07) 4681 2411 - 26/08/2011
