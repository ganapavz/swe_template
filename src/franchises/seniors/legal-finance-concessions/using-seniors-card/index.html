Using your Seniors Card

Where to use your Seniors Card

You can use your Queensland Seniors Card, Seniors Card +go or Seniors Business Discount Card at thousands of participating businesses throughout Queensland.

If you hold a Queensland Seniors Card or Seniors Card +go, you can also access concession fares on public transport in Queensland and interstate.

Finding discounts and special offers

Use the online discount directory to search for Queensland discounts by card type, location, or discount category.

Each year, a Business Discount Directory for Seniors and Carers is produced for the following regions:

  • Greater Brisbane
  • Central Queensland and Wide Bay
  • Far North Queensland
  • Moreton
  • North Queensland and Whitsundays
  • South West Queensland and Darling Downs
  • Sunshine Coast
  • Gold Coast

To register to receive the directory annually by post, replace a lost directory, or order a directory for another region, email cardservices@smartservice.qld.gov.au or call 13 QGOV (13 74 68).

To order a copy of an interstate directory, contact the relevant interstate Seniors Card office.

Using your card overseas

Look for the “SuperGold Cards Welcome” sticker in New Zealand businesses and ask if they also accept Queensland Seniors Card, Seniors +go and Seniors Business Discount Cards.

Queensland and New Zealand governments signed a reciprocal agreement for seniors’ discounts in 2012. Seniors Card, Seniors Card +go and Seniors Business Discount Card holders can now receive discounts and special offers on goods and services from businesses participating in the agreement. The agreement does not include government concessions.

Search the SuperGold online discount directory or look for the “SuperGold Cards Welcome” stickers when travelling in New Zealand, and ask the salesperson if they also accept the Queensland Seniors Card, Seniors +go or Seniors Business Discount Card. If a business does not offer discounts for Australian seniors, this will be noted on their website under ‘exclusions’.

The SuperGold Card site has the most up-to-date information about the New Zealand SuperGold Card discount scheme.

Seniors travelling to Queensland from overseas can search the online discount directory to find businesses offering discounts to visiting overseas cardholders.

How to use your Seniors Card

Shopping for goods and services

  • Ask the salesperson if they provide discounts to seniors.
  • Make the salesperson aware you are a card holder before
    • negotiating the price
    • requesting a quote
    • ordering goods and/or services
    • paying for goods and/or services.
    • If you have difficulty obtaining a discount from a participating business, call Card Services at Smart Service Queensland on (07) 3224 8747

Other concessions (Seniors Card and Seniors Card +go only)

  • The Queensland Government offers concessions on services such as transport, motor vehicle registration, health, recreation and education.
  • Concessions are also available on electricity supply. You will need to speak with your service provider about individual discounts.

Recommending a business

If you know a business in your area that might like to join the Seniors Business Discount Card scheme, email businessdirectory@smartservice.qld.gov.au or contact us on (07) 3224 8747.

We can approach the business on your behalf to invite them to participate in the scheme.

Acknowledging outstanding customer service from a business

You can acknowledge outstanding service by nominating your favourite business.

The business must participate in the Seniors Business Discount Card scheme. Nominations can be made by using 1 of the ways below:

  • complete a nomination form*
  • emailing Card Services on businessdirectory@smartservice.qld.gov.au
  • calling Card Services on (07) 3224 8747.

*tear out the nomination form from your business discount directory, or download and print a copy:

  • nomination form (PDF, 163 KB)
  • nomination form (RTF, 30KB)

You can nominate 1 business per year and any nominations received after the closure date on the form will be considered in the following year.

To provide feedback about a participating business, email businessdirectory@smartservice.qld.gov.au, or phone 13 QGOV (13 7468) or (07) 3224 8747. If required, we will follow up and let you know the outcome.

Handy hint

If you lose your Business Discount Directory for Seniors and Carers or would like to request a different region’s directory—email cardservices@smartservice.qld.gov.au for your free copy. You can also phone 13 QGOV (13 74 68) and order one over the phone.
