Sister states

A Sister State relationship is an agreement to encourage cooperation and cultural exchange between states.

Our sister states are:

  • Province of Central Java, Republic of Indonesia
  • Province of Kyonggi, Republic of Korea
  • Prefecture of Saitama, Japan
  • Municipality of Shanghai, People's Republic of China
  • State of South Carolina, United States of America
