When you think your child has a disability

There’s been no diagnosis but you have a gut feeling: your child is ‘different’. It’s not just that your child isn’t keeping up with other babies or toddlers: it’s that something just doesn’t feel right.

Believe in yourself. Don’t dismiss your feelings. As the saying goes, ‘don’t leave a stone unturned’ in searching out what might be behind that gut feeling. The first thing you need to do is make an appointment with your doctor to request a referral to a paediatrician or specialist.
