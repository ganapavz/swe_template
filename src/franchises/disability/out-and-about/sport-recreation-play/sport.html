Sport

The Disability Online sport guide includes information about topics for people with a disability; their families, carers and friends; as well as information for coaches and clubs about promoting and supporting inclusion in sport and sports and recreation funding.

It includes practical advice to help you get involved with sport and get active in other ways; as well as stories shared by Queenslanders with a disability about their experiences with participating in social and elite sport.
