Buy your JP and Cdec products online

As a Justice of the Peace (JP) or Commissioner for Declarations (Cdec), you need items that identify you so that the public knows the important role you carry out in our community. Products can also help you to perform your JP or Cdec tasks.

You should use this online shop to select and pay for your JP and Cdec products. Your items will be delivered within 15 business days.
