Government service counter locations

Please note:

  • This listing contains mostly regional and remote service counters only. It is not a complete listing of all service counters (such as police stations, licence testing counters, etc).
  • opening hours and services available vary. Unexpected closures may not be immediately reflected here. You may wish to contact a service counter to confirm opening hours and services available.

If you live in a large metropolitan area (such as Brisbane, Caloundra, Cairns etc), you can:

  • go to the website of the department that provides the service you’re looking for
  • search for a service counter if you have a transport or vehicle query.
