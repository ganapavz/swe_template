Emergency contacts

  • If you are in immediate danger, phone the police on Triple Zero (000).
  • For help and advice call DVConnect ( http://www.dvconnect.org/ ) Womensline on 1800 811 811 (24 hours, 7 days a week) or Mensline on 1800 600 636 (9am to midnight, 7 days a week).
  • If you or someone you know has experienced elder abuse, phone the Elder Abuse Helpline ( http://www.eapu.com.au/ ) on 1300 651 192 for help and advice.
  • If you have experienced sexual abuse, phone the Sexual Assault Helpline ( http://www.dvconnect.org/?page_id=342 ) on 1800 010 120.
